Meet the Gang

Judy Anderson

Tenor

Judy loves singing and playing all types of music and has collected quite a music library. When she is not singing, Judy enjoys gardening, playing golf, and keeping the rest of us in line.


Michelle Stiller

Tenor

Michelle is a member of four different singing groups, including the acclaimed Symphony New Hampshire Chorus and the a capella group Colla Voce. We don't know how she keeps all of the music straight, but if she starts singing Handel during a barbershop rift, you'll know why.


John Jackson

Lead

John is our resident poet and is always up for a good lyric or two, particularly if it involves harbor seals.


Kurt Staven

Baritone/Bass

Kurt first sang in a barbershop quartet while in high school. He no longer needs a barber, but the music lingers on. Kurt's interests include castles, cartoons, and cryptozoology. Kurt is a xenophile.


Dave Hill

Baritone/Bass

Dave has been singing barbershop and choral music off and on since college. If you see him driving with his mouth wide open, don't be alarmed - he's just singing.

Alan Hoff

Bass

Alan is our latest member, joining just in time as we bring out all of our really complicated choreography, so if we turn left and he turns right, take it easy on him.

Jim Oehler

Bass

Originally from Wisconsin, Jim is a huge Packers fan, but we don't hold that against him. After all, that SuperBowl against the Patriots in 1995 was a long time ago.
